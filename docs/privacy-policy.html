Choppy Wood - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Overview

Choppy Wood is a simple arcade game that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

Local Storage

The app stores your high score locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages and does not collect any data from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
